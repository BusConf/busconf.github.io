The Ultimate FP Unconference

BusConf is all about community: We want to provide a platform where people interested in Functional Programming can meet to learn, teach, discuss, write code and have a good time.

  • First Open Space Unconference focusing on Functional Programming.
  • Low cost, non-profit event.
  • 48 hours of learning, teaching, discussions and fun
  • Nice venue close to the Frankfurt airport

If you have not been to an open space event before, make sure to check out Format to get an idea what it is all about. Tickets are not yet on sale but you can follow us on twitter to receive updates on ticket sales.
